Our Objective.

To upskill, network, & connect the millions of unemployed African college graduates with the job opportunities they need, and provide companies with access and visibility to the high-skilled talent-pool needed to meet their business goals on the continent.

The Problem.

50% of the 10 million annual college graduates in Sub-Saharan Africa over the past decade were unable to find jobs sometimes 5-years after graduating from University

Meanwhile, the continent produced 10 million jobs per year within the same period and had a predicted shortage of 31 million mid-high skill workers at the end of the decade.

Our Vision.

We aim to become Africa's #1 talent source by annually connecting 5 million job-seekers with high-skill jobs in and outside SSA by 2040
