About Us

To upskill, network and connect the millions of unemployed African college graduates with the job opportunities they need, and provide companies with access and visibility to the high-skilled talent-pool needed to meet their business goals on the continent.

Our Team
Founder & CEO
Habib Bangura

Is a senior level executive with 10+ years of experience and expertise in designing & scaling high impact education and human capital ventures in the US. In the past 2 years alone he has informed education policy & funding bills in 3 states while launching & scaling an education non-profit across 6 different states, partnering with 50+ school districts; 140+ schools, directly impacting over 40 thousand students.

As Chief Executive Officer, he shepherds the vision & strategic direction of our organization including sourcing partnerships & managing our growth strategy.

Habib holds graduate degrees from Harvard & Johns Hopkins University and has an obsessive commitment to marshalling and remitting the collective talent of the global black diaspora towards human capital improvement in Africa.

VP Product & Technology

Has deep experience in the African Startup ecosystem, most recently helping to launch 2 successful tech startup on the continent. He's an FinTech expert with extensive experience advising firms in banking, healthcare, and auto industries across the continental US, Europe & Africa.

Alhassan holds a BA in Political Science, a Master's in International Relations as well as post graduate and executive level certificates in International Economics and Strategic Financial Evaluation from American University, Georgetown University and Harvard Business School.
